Hero carousel assets — Landing page.

The featured carousel near the top of the toko.app landing page. Unlike the Launchpad/Marketplace banner, this one is filled automatically from featured secondary-market listings — so the “asset” is your listing’s image. This page tells you how to make it look great there.

Featured card · 4:3~4 minSeller / creator

The landing-page hero is a portrait-ish 4:3 card, not a full-width banner. It rotates a small set of featured highlights pulled from the secondary market, weighted so listings with a higher referral fee are more likely to appear. You don't upload a banner for this surface — Toko uses your listing / collection image — so the job here is to make that image read well inside the 4:3 frame.

Where this content comes from

The landing hero is not hand-picked. It samples from live featured listings and biases toward higher seller-set referral fees (the 1–10% placement fee). Paying to feature is what earns eligibility and lifts your odds of appearing — there's no manual “what do we highlight” step. Full rules live in the Landing page design and Secondary marketplace specs.

The frame & safe zone
The whole 4:3 image shows in a dark rounded card. Toko overlays a badge (Live / Ending soon), the “Featured drop” eyebrow, and the collection title across the bottom ~35% on a scrim. Keep that band clear and put your subject in the upper/centre.

If your token art is square

Token art is usually 1:1, but this frame is 4:3. A square image is centre-cropped to fit, trimming the top and bottom. Either supply a dedicated 4:3 featured image, or keep your square composition centred with breathing room so the crop doesn't clip the subject.

Composition

  • Subject upper / centre. The bottom third is covered by the badge, eyebrow, and title — keep faces and key detail above it.
  • Strong, simple, high-contrast. The card is small and placement is automatic and random within the featured pool; a bold, legible image at a glance wins.
  • Readable behind white text. The title is white on a bottom scrim — a darker or calmer lower area helps it pop.
  • No baked-in words. The collection name and status badge are drawn live; don't duplicate them in the art.

How to appear here (and rank higher)

  • List on the secondary market and opt the listing into featured placement.
  • Set your referral fee (1–10%). A higher fee increases your likelihood of appearing in the landing hero and your placement priority. See The Marketplace, explained.
  • Give it a great 4:3-friendly image per the spec above — that's the part you control here.

Do

  • Supply a 4:3 image at 1600 × 1200+.
  • Keep the subject upper / centre.
  • Leave the bottom third calm for the overlay.
  • Use sRGB WebP/JPG under 400 KB.

Don't

  • Bake the title or a status badge into the image.
  • Put key detail in the bottom third.
  • Assume a square crop is safe — it isn't.
  • Rely on fine detail; the card is small.

Export checklist

  • 4:3 image — 1600 × 1200 (retina 2048 × 1536), sRGB.
  • Subject upper / centre; bottom ~35% kept clear.
  • No baked-in title or badge.
  • WebP / JPG q80–85, ≤ 400 KB.
  • Listing opted into featured, with a referral fee set to lift placement.
