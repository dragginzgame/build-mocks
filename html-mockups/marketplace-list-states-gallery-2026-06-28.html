List for sale — states gallery

The seller flow for the v1 marketplace: a holder lists a token they own at a fixed price. They set how much and in which currency (from Toko's curated whitelist), pick a preset duration (max 14 days), and set a referral fee (a 1–10% slider, min 1%). An unsold listing simply returns to the wallet. The token being listed is shown the whole way through. The referral fee (the secondary-sale fee — today always paid to the Toko Marketplace; higher fee = higher placement) is charged only on a completed sale. No auctions, no offers in v1. Source: Design Documentation/secondary-marketplace-design.md.

Reached from My Tokens → List for sale. Related: Marketplace · Token detail
