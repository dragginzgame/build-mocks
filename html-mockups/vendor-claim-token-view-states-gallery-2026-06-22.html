Toko · Launchpad · consistency reference

Vendor claim — token view.

The buyer-facing token view reached from a vendor's claim page (Launchpad → vendor detail → token). It's the same read-only minted-token viewer used in the wallet and creator inventory — large asset gallery, creator-authored description, rarity, attributes, provenance — but paired with a claim panel instead of owner actions: vendor identity, cost, live availability, eligibility, and the claim CTA. Generator internals (seed, batch) stay creator-only. CTA states follow the Launchpad spec: Claim now · View requirements · Sold out · Coming soon.

States: Available & eligible · Requirements not met · Sold out · Coming soon · Claim confirm · Result
01Available & eligible — Claim nowA live vendor, the viewer on the left and the claim panel on the right. The panel carries the vendor identity, the cost, a live availability bar (claimed vs supply), the eligibility summary (here: open to all), and the primary Claim now CTA. Eligibility shown here is a cached hint — it's always re-validated at claim time before settlement.
