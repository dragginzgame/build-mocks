Project Billing — cycles management · states gallery

The project overview's Billing tab (renamed 2026-07-02 from "Cycles" — billing owns cycles management, plus the storage/Cashier billing shown in the project gallery). In V1 cycles are self-funded from the project's own treasury: all claim payments flow into the treasury, a locked percentage is reserved for cycles, and on every inflow — plus an hourly check — the reserve is topped up by converting funds to ICP via an AMM and buying cycles. Only the excess above the reserve can be withdrawn. Anyone logged in can top a project up via its public link. Follows the page shell: white for the breadcrumb bar and cards only; header on grey.

Source: Design Documentation/project-treasury-and-cycles-design.md. Related: Burn & Reputation · Revenue presets.
