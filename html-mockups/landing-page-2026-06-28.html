FAQ

Good to know.

What is Toko?+
Toko is a platform to launch, collect and trade digital collections. Creators build and distribute collections; collectors discover drops, claim tokens, and trade them on the marketplace.
What's the difference between Launchpad and Marketplace?+
Launchpad is for primary distribution - live and upcoming drops you claim directly from a creator's vendor. The Marketplace is for secondary trading - buying and selling tokens at a fixed price after a drop has happened.
Do I need to connect a wallet to browse?+
No. You can browse drops, collections and the marketplace freely. You only connect a wallet when you want to claim, buy, sell, or create.
How do I become a creator?+
Creator Studio is currently available to approved creators only as we open the platform gradually. Request creator access and we'll be in touch - once approved, you can set up a collection, configure a vendor, and launch it on the Launchpad.
Where does community discussion happen?+
Live discussion is on our Discord at discord.gg/toko. Guides (knowledge base), answers (FAQ) and updates (blog) all live here on the Toko website.
Where can I read the terms and privacy policy?+
Links to our Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy are in the footer below and are kept up to date as policies evolve.
