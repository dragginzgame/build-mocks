Pricing lifecycle — decoupled gates (R81)

Ruled 2026-07-22 (raised by Gabriel): collection publishing is decoupled from distribution pricing. Collection defaults are now optional templates — a collection reaches Review/Live with no claim costs configured, and its status checklist shows cost coverage informationally only. The gate moved to the vendor: a Draft vendor may be unpriced, but before its Review/Live every tier must resolve to a cost on the vendor's own copy (own entry, or R61 tier-to-tier inheritance; free counts; R73 full tier set). Vendor gate copy: Reviewed, stocked and priced. Unset ≠ Free — a vendor created without defaults starts unset, never silently free.

Change spec + GitHub-ready task list: Design Documentation/pricing-lifecycle-decoupling.md. Reframed galleries: Collection defaults · Vendor management.
