FAQ

Good to know.

What is Toko?+
Toko is a platform to launch, collect and trade digital collections. Creators build and distribute collections; collectors discover drops, claim tokens, and trade them on the marketplace.
What's the difference between Launchpad and Marketplace?+
Launchpad is for primary distribution — live and upcoming drops you claim directly from a creator's vendor. The Marketplace is for secondary trading — buying and selling tokens after a drop has happened.
Do I need to connect a wallet to browse?+
No. You can browse drops, collections and the marketplace freely. You only connect a wallet when you want to claim, buy, sell, or create.
How do creators launch a collection?+
In Creator Studio you set up the collection (attributes, rarity, supply), configure a vendor with claim rules and revenue split, submit it for approval, and launch it on the Launchpad.
How are vendor claims moderated?+
Every vendor must be approved by Toko before it can run. Claim rules and settlement are enforced by the platform, and activity is auditable.
