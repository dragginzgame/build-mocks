Going Live

Admin and above — Admin, Treasurer, Super Admin, and the Creator — can set a token, collection, or vendor Live. Maintainers can build everything but can't take it Live.

Managing access

Only the Creator and Super Admin can change who has access. The Creator role is unique: only the Creator can hand it to someone else, and they're demoted to Super Admin when they do.

Bring your team in.

Permissions decide who can do what in this project. Add a collaborator by principal and pick a role — they'll inherit access to everything inside the project.

Two groups of roles

Administrative & bulk roles.

Administrative roles are assigned to people. Creator is the unique project owner with full rights and the only one who can transfer ownership. Super Admin manages access; Treasurer adds funding and withdrawals; Admin has full admin rights but can't manage users; Maintainer builds tokens, assets, and generators.

Bulk roles attach to a whitelist, not individuals. Trusted can use live generators and pin assets for review; Reviewer gets a read-only preview of unreleased work. Roles are fixed for now and apply to the whole project.
