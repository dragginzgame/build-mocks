Tokens — Live.

The Live view of the token list (reached from the Live tab) is a read-only viewer — selecting a token opens its locked definition, and Mint opens the mint modals. Live token definitions are final within their supply policy: the locked definition (no edits, no deletion), a single mint, minting a stack of copies in one go, and the fully-minted state where supply is exhausted.

06Mint budgets — per tierPart of the Live management view. Supply is frozen at collection go-live, but the roster keeps growing — so creators need a persistent read on each tier. Per tier: budget · minted · remaining · potential demand (the copies live definitions could still mint). When potential exceeds the remaining budget the tier is oversubscribed: only a fraction can ever be minted, first-come, so the choice of what to mint matters. Weighted shows one row per tier; Uniform / Tiered show a single overall budget row.

07Mint blocked — tier budget exhaustedDistinct from "Max already minted" (frame 05), which is the token's own cap.
