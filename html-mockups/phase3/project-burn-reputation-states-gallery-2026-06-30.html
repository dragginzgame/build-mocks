Project Burn & Reputation — states gallery

The project overview's Burn / Reputation tab — a coming-soon feature: in the current nav this tab renders disabled with a "Soon" chip; this gallery shows the future shipped state (tab nav = the style guide's Tabs component, no Media tab — media lives on Details). Toko Reputation is the cumulative $TOKO the project has burned — it gates Project Mastery unlocks. Most is earned automatically (every claim routes a min-1% share to a Toko account that converts it to $TOKO and burns it); the rest is bought by burning $TOKO from a wallet, tracked separately. Reputation is project-level (shared by the team). Follows the page shell: white for the breadcrumb bar and cards only; header on grey.

Source: Design Documentation/collection-archetype-implementation-plan.md, project-studio-design.md. Related: Project Mastery · Revenue presets.
