Toko · Consistency reference

Tokens — Manage minted.

The collection-held minted inventory and every operational action on it: browse and filter held copies, open a single copy's full read-only detail, and move to a vendor, transfer, burn, or run stack actions — single and bulk. Once a copy is moved to a vendor, sold, or transferred out it leaves this list.

Every copy shares the token definition's single issue number (e.g. #7) and is told apart only by its copy number (Copy 5 / 100) — copies never get their own issue number, and burning or transferring a copy never changes it.
