Project — Revenue presets.

A project-level library of reusable revenue presets — how claim and sale proceeds are split. Laid out like Vendors: a list of named presets, each with a Draft / Review / Live status and Edit / Delete. A preset sets the fees (a mandatory Toko burn, min 1%, that builds project reputation) and royalties to beneficiaries. A vendor selects both a Claim preset (claim proceeds: Toko burn + beneficiaries) and a Sale preset (the secondary split — royalties ≤ 3% only; no project/cycles fee, no Toko burn, no referral). Cycles are not a per-claim % — the project self-funds them from its own treasury (surfaced on the Cycles tab). Sale presets replace the old collection "Sale defaults" surface.

Design note (not UI)Revenue presets are project-scoped and reusable; a vendor selects a Live preset at creation, which copies its values onto the vendor (so later edits to the preset don't change existing vendors). Lifecycle mirrors other project policy: editable in Draft/Review, locked / view-only at Live while in use. Constraints: mandatory Toko burn 1–10% (min 1%), each royalty 0.1–10%, total royalties ≤ 20% on claim. Cycles are self-funded from the project treasury (surfaced on the Cycles tab), not a per-claim %. Taper (No taper / Cliff / Linear / Curve) and time period (1/3/6/9/12/24 months) only apply to fixed-period royalties; "Forever" royalties are constant. Sale presets carry royalties ≤ 3% total only (beneficiaries; no project/cycles fee, no Toko burn, no referral); a vendor selects both a Claim and a Sale preset. They replace the old collection "Sale defaults" surface.
